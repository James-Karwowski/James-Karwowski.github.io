Housing

Our housing program provides safe and stable living spaces for individuals and families transitioning out of homelessness. We offer both temporary shelters and long-term housing assistance, helping clients find affordable, permanent homes. By combining access to shelter with job readiness programs and financial counseling, we empower residents to achieve self-sufficiency and long-term stability.

Food Pantry

The food pantry ensures that no one in our community goes hungry. Stocked with fresh produce, pantry staples, and hygiene essentials, it serves hundreds of individuals each week. We partner with local farms, restaurants, and donors to provide balanced, nutritious meals while reducing food waste. Our volunteers also deliver meals directly to those unable to visit in person, ensuring consistent access to nourishment and care.
